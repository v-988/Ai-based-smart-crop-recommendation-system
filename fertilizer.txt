Fertilizer Usage Guide

Fertilizers help increase crop yield.

Nitrogen Fertilizers:
Promote leaf growth.
Examples: Urea, Ammonium sulfate.

Phosphorus Fertilizers:
Help root development.
Examples: DAP, SSP.

Potassium Fertilizers:
Improve plant strength.
Examples: MOP, SOP.

Organic Fertilizers:
Compost, manure, vermicompost.

Fertilizer Tips:
Do soil test before applying
Do not overuse fertilizers
Apply in correct season
Mix with irrigation
Use organic fertilizers when possible

Crop Specific Fertilizer Use:
Rice — high nitrogen
Wheat — balanced NPK
Vegetables — more potassium
Fruits — phosphorus important

Safe Usage:
Wear gloves
Avoid direct contact
Store in dry place
Keep away from children
