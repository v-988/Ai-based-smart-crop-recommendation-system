Crop Growing Guidelines

Rice:
Needs high water and clay soil. Best temperature 20–35°C.

Wheat:
Needs cool climate. Grows well in loamy soil.

Maize:
Needs moderate rainfall. Suitable for well-drained soil.

Cotton:
Grows well in black soil. Needs warm climate.

Sugarcane:
Needs heavy water and fertile soil.

Groundnut:
Grows in sandy loam soil. Needs less water.

Millets:
Suitable for dry regions. Needs less rainfall.

Pulses:
Improve soil nitrogen. Good for crop rotation.

Vegetables:
Need loamy soil and regular watering.

Crop Selection Tips:
Choose crop based on rainfall
Check soil type
Check temperature range
Use seasonal crops
Avoid growing same crop repeatedly
