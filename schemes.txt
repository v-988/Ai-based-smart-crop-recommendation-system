Farmer Support Schemes in India

PM-KISAN Scheme:
Provides income support to small and marginal farmers.
Financial assistance given yearly.

Crop Insurance Scheme:
Protects farmers from crop loss due to natural disasters.

Soil Health Card Scheme:
Provides soil testing and fertilizer recommendation.

Irrigation Support Scheme:
Helps farmers install drip and sprinkler systems.

Subsidy for Farm Equipment:
Government gives subsidy on tractors and tools.

Organic Farming Support:
Financial help for organic farming practices.

Seed Subsidy Programs:
Certified seeds available at reduced price.

Farmer Loan Schemes:
Low interest agricultural loans available.

How to Apply:
Visit nearest agriculture office
Use online farmer portals
Submit land and identity documents
Check eligibility before applying
